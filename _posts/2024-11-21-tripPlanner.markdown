## what makes it different?

Other powerful trip planner like Wanderlog can provide wonderful functions for trip planning including navigation, hotel reservation and so on.  

Unlike other powerful trip planner, my trip planner just provide a function like TO-DO LIST.

You plan it, you do it. that is easy. Don't get swept away by the information storm.

Just envision yourself as an 'I' person, an introvert who prefers minimal distractions from the overwhelming influx of information. You seek a tranquil destination, a place to simply soak in the breathtaking views. This app is perfectly tailored for your needs.
